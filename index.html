Project Overview

Understanding Microbial Worlds

Welcome to the Bacterial Genomics Project. Our mission is to decode the genetic blueprints of bacterial life to advance scientific knowledge and apply it to medicine, agriculture, and industry. This project is an open collaboration space for scientists, researchers, and enthusiasts around the globe.
